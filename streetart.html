Streetart Amsterdam

Street Art

For street art, as we know it today, we will have to flash forward to the latter half of the century. Just like it was in the first half of the century we see a rise in street art in the poorer neighbourhoods of the city.

NDSM: graffiti art.

With a free ferry behind Amsterdam Central Station you can go to the former harbor area on the other side of the IJ. The NDSM neighborhood is known for graffiti art. New pieces are often added so that you are amazed every time, read more.

Street Art Platanenweg

Ten murals by 10 artists give color to five flats in Amsterdam East. Every artist has his own style. The overarching theme of these works of art is 'Amsterdam for everyone', Read more.

Safety - Alaniz

The artist Alaniz was commissioned to make a mural on an apartment complex on Dirk Sonoystraat. The theme should be 'security'. It's about protecting the weakest, even when you're in danger yourself, read more.

Van Gogh hat

The Van Gogh hat by Sandrine Boulet is part of the 'Office Walk with a Twist' collection. This artwork was made from a car threshold in the Schiphol parking garage around the BASE building, read more.

Amsterdam West: puppies

Stroll through the parks of Amsterdam. Here you will find many special creations. Such as the super-sized puppy sculptures at the Postejeweg. These cheerful creatures are the work of Marjolijn Mandersloot, read more.
